**Headline Idea: 🎓 Transforming Campus Management with AI & MERN!**

I’m excited to share my latest project, **CampusConnect** — a smart, AI-driven portal designed to streamline campus security and student services. 🚀

As campuses grow, so does the need for secure, efficient identity verification and digital transactions. I built CampusConnect to address these challenges using a modern full-stack approach and AI integration.

### 🌟 Key Highlights:
*   **AI Face Recognition**: Secure, biometric-based identity verification with liveness detection to prevent spoofing.
*   **Digital e-ID System**: Dynamic identity cards generated only after successful AI verification.
*   **Smart Wallet**: Integrated payment system for campus services and fines, powered by Razorpay.
*   **Admin Power**: A unified dashboard for student management, approvals, and real-time announcements.

### 🛠️ The Tech Stack:
*   **Frontend**: React.js (Vite) + Vanilla CSS (Glassmorphism UI)
*   **Backend**: Node.js & Express
*   **Database**: MongoDB Atlas
*   **AI Service**: Python (Flask) + DeepFace models (Hosted on Hugging Face)
*   **Deployment**: Vercel & Render
